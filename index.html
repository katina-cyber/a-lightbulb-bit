A bit

A "bit" in terms of Computer Science is simply something that can have one of two states. In your computer, a bit can have the value of either a "1" or a "0", but we can look at other examples of what would fit the definition of a bit. Another example of a bit would be a light, which can be either "on" or "off".

If you have ever flicked a light switch in your home, or turned a lamp on or off, then you have seen an example of a bit in action! Using lots of these "bits" we can lay the foundation for our computers so we can do cool stuff with them!
